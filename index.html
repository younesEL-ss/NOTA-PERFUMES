Our Story

Born in the Heart of Fes

Nota Perfumes was born from a deep love for the ancient art of perfumery — a craft woven into the very soul of Fes Medina, Morocco. In the winding alleys of this UNESCO World Heritage city, our story began.

Every fragrance we create is a tribute to Moroccan heritage — the rose fields of Kelaat MGoun, the oud markets of the ancient souks, the spice-laden air of the tanneries. We source only the finest natural ingredients.

At Nota Perfumes, a true fragrance is not merely a scent — it is an emotion, a memory, a journey. Each bottle is an invitation to explore the rich tapestry of Moroccan culture.
